Introduction

I am Shahriyer Sayem, also known as Utso. I am an undergraduate student in Electrical and Electronic Engineering, and I spend a lot of my time exploring graphic design, photography, video production, and web design.

I enjoy turning ideas into real work, whether it is a design, a photo, or a small project. I am still exploring different directions and learning as I go.

This website is a way for me to share my work and document my journey.

Open to collaborations, freelance work, and mentorship. Reach out if you want to connect.

Future Goals

I am focused on building a strong foundation in electrical and electronic engineering as I begin my undergraduate journey. My primary interest lies in the power sector, with a long term goal of pursuing higher studies and keeping the path open toward research and academia.

At the same time, I continue exploring design, photography, video production, and web development as creative outlets. These allow me to express ideas visually and build practical projects. I plan to take on freelance work in my free time while continuing personal projects.

I also aim to grow this website into a structured archive of my work, where I document projects, share selected writings, and track my progress over time. In the long run, I want to build a balanced path where engineering remains my core focus, while creativity supports how I think and communicate ideas.
